SNCF Optimize your time

Transilien is the name of the commuter train network of the National Society of French Railways (SNCF) train stations serving mainly the Ile-de-France. They asked us to solve one of their biggest problems : the rush hours. So we created this webApp which proposes to the user some activities, according to his profil, to optimize his time and wait for the end of the peak hours.
